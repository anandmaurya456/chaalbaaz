# Privacy Policy

**Chaalbaaz — AI Chess Move Prediction**
**Last Updated:** February 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Chaalbaaz** ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Chaalbaaz application — an AI-powered chess assistant designed for learning and practice purposes.

By using Chaalbaaz, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the application.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Chess.com Username** — to fetch your game history and statistics via Chess.com's public API.
- **Account preferences** — settings such as preferred analysis depth, notification preferences, and display options.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Chess game data** — game histories, PGN (Portable Game Notation) data, and board positions fetched from Chess.com's public API.
- **Usage data** — features accessed, session duration, and interaction patterns within the app.
- **Device and technical information** — browser type, operating system, IP address, and application version for debugging and performance purposes.

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do **not** collect Chess.com account passwords or authentication credentials.
- We do **not** collect payment information.
- We do **not** collect sensitive personal information such as government IDs, financial data, or health information.
- We do **not** track users across third-party websites.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

- **Providing the service** — analysing board positions and generating move suggestions using the AI engine.
- **Personalisation** — tailoring move suggestions based on your game history, playing style, and ELO rating.
- **Improving the application** — understanding usage patterns to fix bugs and enhance features.
- **Communication** — sending important updates about the application (only if you opt in).

---

## 4. Chess.com Data Usage

Chaalbaaz accesses Chess.com data strictly through **Chess.com's official public API**. We:

- Do **not** scrape Chess.com's website.
- Do **not** store Chess.com login credentials.
- Do **not** interfere with or automate live rated games.
- Only access **publicly available** game and player statistics.

Use of Chess.com data is subject to [Chess.com's Terms of Service](https://www.chess.com/legal/user-agreement).

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored securely on **Microsoft Azure** infrastructure.
- We implement industry-standard encryption (TLS/HTTPS) for all data in transit.
- Stored data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption.
- Access to user data is restricted to authorised personnel only.
- We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service.

---

## 6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do **not** sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

- **Chess.com API** — to fetch game data on your behalf.
- **Microsoft Azure** — as our cloud infrastructure provider.
- **Legal obligations** — if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests.
